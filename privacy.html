Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 7, 2025

1. Who we are

Jeth Enterprise Drycleaning (“JE Drycleaning”, “we”, “our”, “us”) provides garment-care services. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website or services.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details: name, phone number, email, address.
  • Order details: items, quantities, delivery preferences, totals.
  • Payment information: processed by our payment partner (Paystack); we do not store card numbers.
  • Technical data: basic analytics or logs (IP address, device, browser) to secure and improve the service.

3. How we use your information

  • To process orders, pickups, and deliveries.
  • To communicate updates, invoices, and support messages (including via WhatsApp).
  • To improve our services, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • With your consent, to send occasional promotions you can opt out of anytime.

4. Sharing

We share information with service providers who help us operate our business—e.g., Paystack (payments), EmailJS (email delivery), and Google Apps Script/Sheets (order logging)—only as needed to perform their services and subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Data retention

We retain records for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal/accounting requirements, then securely delete or anonymize them.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including the Nigeria Data Protection Act/NDPR), you may request access, correction, deletion, or withdraw consent. Contact us using the details below.

8. Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. Where analytics are used, we will provide notices and choices as required.

9. International transfers

Our service providers may process data in other countries. We rely on appropriate safeguards as permitted by law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated where required.
